Soccer Scholarships in the USA

Soccer is already causing a stir in the United States, and American coaches are looking for skilled players from abroad to offer soccer scholarships in the USA.

The lifestyle of a college soccer player is similar to that of a professional player, as they train in excellent facilities and compete against the best teams in the country.

Frequently Asked Questions

The higher the soccer level of a candidate, the more interest American coaches will have, and the greater the amount of soccer scholarships in the USA. For this reason, the current competitive sports level and the results obtained in previous years are very relevant when applying for soccer scholarships in the USA.

There are over 1,500 universities with soccer teams, and the vast majority offer sports scholarships. The main organization, the NCAA, has more than 1,000 universities across its divisions.

What Requirements Do You Need to Get a Soccer Scholarship?

Academic Requirements
  • Complete high school.
  • Take the SAT and TOEFL exams.
Sports Requirements
  • High competitive level.
  • Comply with the rules of American university leagues.
