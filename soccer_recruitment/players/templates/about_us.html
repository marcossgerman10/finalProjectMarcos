About Soccer Abroad

Your gateway to soccer opportunities in the U.S. while pursuing your academic goals.

About the Creator

This platform was created by me, Marcos German. I am currently pursuing an MBA in Information Systems. Before starting my MBA, I completed a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I created Soccer Abroad because I wanted to share the unique opportunity I had to study abroad while also pursuing my passion for soccer. Through scholarships, I was able to continue playing the sport I love while studying in the U.S., and I want to help others do the same.

My goal with this platform is to provide others with the resources and guidance they need to pursue a similar path—balancing academics with athletics. Whether you’re aiming for a career in soccer or pursuing higher education, this platform is here to help you achieve your goals.

If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to reach out to me. You can contact me directly at marcoss.german@gmail.com.
